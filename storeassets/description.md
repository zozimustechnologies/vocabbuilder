## Full Description

**VocabBuilder** is a privacy-first vocabulary learning extension that lives in your browser's side panel. Using scientifically proven spaced repetition, it serves you one word at a time and schedules reviews at the optimal moment — so words move from short-term to long-term memory with minimal effort.

### How it works

Each word is shown once per day. You respond with **"Got It"** (you knew it) or **"Not Yet"** (you need more practice). Based on your response, the word is scheduled for review at a progressive interval:

**1 → 3 → 7 → 14 → 30 days**

Once a word completes the full cycle, it's marked as learned. Words you struggle with keep coming back until they stick.

### Key Features

**📚 4 Difficulty Levels**
Choose the level that matches your goals:
- **Simple** — everyday English vocabulary
- **Medium** — professional and academic words
- **Complex** — advanced literary and technical vocabulary
- **Competitive** — GMAT, GRE, LSAT, and CAT exam-level words

**🔥 Streak & Badge System**
- Daily streak counter with fire badge motivation
- Streak milestone badges at 3, 7, 30, and 365 days
- Word-learned lifetime badges (10, 50, 100, 500 words)
- Monthly challenge badges to keep you consistent

**📊 Progress Dashboard**
Track your current streak, longest streak, total words learned, daily goal completion, and monthly word totals — all in one place.

**⚡ Daily Reminders**
Smart browser notifications remind you to review at 9 AM if you haven't opened the extension yet. Build vocabulary as a daily habit, not a chore.

**🧠 Rich Word Cards**
Every word card includes:
- IPA phonetic pronunciation
- Part of speech
- Clear definition with usage context
- Etymology (word origin)
- Synonyms and antonyms
- Example sentence

**🔒 100% Local — No Accounts, No Tracking**
All your progress, word history, streaks, and badges are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. No data is collected, transmitted, or shared. No account required.

### Who is VocabBuilder for?

- **Exam aspirants** preparing for GMAT, GRE, LSAT, CAT, or similar competitive exams
- **ESL learners** building academic and everyday English vocabulary
- **Professionals** wanting to sharpen communication and writing skills
- **Lifelong learners** who want a sustainable, low-friction daily vocabulary habit

### Privacy & Permissions

VocabBuilder requests only the permissions it needs:
- **storage** — saves your progress and word data locally in your browser
- **alarms** — schedules daily review reminders
- **notifications** — sends the daily reminder notification
- **sidePanel** — opens the extension in the browser sidebar

No browsing data, no personal information, no external tracking. The only external request made is to the free [Free Dictionary API](https://dictionaryapi.dev/) when looking up word details — no user data is sent.
